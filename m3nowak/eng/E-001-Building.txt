[head=2]3) Authorisation[/head]

All construction projects require a stamp of the Chief Engineer and signature of the applicant. 
If construction is to be done in a department other than Engineering or in common areas, approval from the head of the department that will be modified is also required.

[head=2]4) Notice[/head]

This permit may be revoked at any time for any reason by the Chief Engineer or higher authority.
This permit does not authorize any unsafe or illegal construction practices.
This permit does not allow the applicant to use any of the engineering resources without explicit permission from the Chief Engineer.
