[head=2]3) Authorisation[/head]

Personal searches require a stamp of the Head of Security, Warden, department head of the subject, or Captain.
Workplace searches require a stamp of the department head of the subject, or Captain.
All searches require a signature of the requesting officer.
The requesting officer cannot authorize a search.

[head=2]4) Notice[/head]

All legal items found during the search must be returned to the subject or left in the workplace.
